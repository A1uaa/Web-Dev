Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4-6
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Butter 1 cup
Sugar 1 cup
Chocolate chips 1 cup
Eggs 2
Baking powder 1 tsp
Vanilla extract 1 tsp

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).

Step 2: Mix butter and sugar until creamy.

Step 3: Add eggs and vanilla, then stir in flour and baking powder.

Step 4: Fold in the chocolate chips.

Step 5: Scoop onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes until golden.
